Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

getPresented. ("we", "us") builds and hosts websites for small businesses across the UK. This page explains what information we collect when you contact us, enquire about a website, or visit this site — and what we do with it.

We've kept this short because we've kept our setup simple: we don't use cookies, we don't run advertising trackers, and we don't sell or share your information with anyone outside the few tools listed below that help us actually deliver the service.

01Cookies

This site does not use cookies. We don't run a cookie banner because there's nothing to consent to — no advertising cookies, no tracking cookies, and no third-party marketing pixels are placed on your device when you browse this site.

02What we collect, and why

We only collect information that you choose to give us, or basic technical data needed to understand how the site is used. We don't collect more than we need.

When What we collect Why
Get started / enquiry form Business name, your name, phone number, email, WhatsApp number (if given), business details, and anything you write in the message or notes fields To understand your business and reply with a quote and next steps
Contact form on a client site Name, email or phone, and your message So the business you're contacting can reply to your enquiry
WhatsApp Your phone number and message content To answer your question or discuss your project
Making a payment Payment details (handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details) To process payment for a website build or hosting
Browsing this site Anonymous, aggregate analytics — page views, general location (country/region level), device type. No individual visitor is identified. To understand which pages are useful and fix anything that isn't working

03The tools we use

We rely on a small number of trusted third-party services to run this site and respond to enquiries. Each only receives the information needed to do its specific job.

FormSubmit — handles form submissions from this site and delivers them straight to our inbox. FormSubmit does not use cookies on our forms.

Stripe — processes all payments. Stripe is PCI-compliant and we never see or store your full card details.

Umami Analytics — a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics tool. It tells us in aggregate how many people visit, which pages they look at, and roughly where from — without identifying individual visitors or tracking anyone across other websites.

Google Workspace / Gmail — we use Gmail to send and receive enquiry emails.

GitHub Pages — hosts this website's files.

We don't use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any advertising or retargeting trackers on this site.

04How long we keep your information

If you enquire but don't go ahead with a website, we'll keep your details for up to 12 months in case you'd like to pick things up again, then delete them. If you become a client, we keep the information needed to run your site and hosting for as long as you're with us, plus a reasonable period afterwards for our own records (typically up to 6 years, in line with standard UK accounting practice).

05Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Ask what we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct anything inaccurate
  • Ask us to delete your information, where we're not required to keep it for legal or accounting reasons
  • Ask us to stop using your information for a particular purpose

To do any of these, just email us — see below. We'll respond within 30 days.

06Who we share information with

We don't sell your information, and we don't share it with advertisers. The only sharing that happens is with the tools listed in section 3, each of which processes data only to help us deliver the service to you. If you're a website client, your contact form submissions go directly to your own inbox, not ours.

07Changes to this policy

If we change how we collect or use information, we'll update this page and change the date at the top. For anything significant, we'll let existing clients know directly.

Questions about your data?

Email us and we'll get back to you — usually within a day.
